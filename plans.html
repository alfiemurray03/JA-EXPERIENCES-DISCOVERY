Social tariff

The JA Experiences & Discovery Social Tariff is intended to support customers who may need lower-cost access to destination discovery and experience guidance. It may be available to customers receiving Universal Credit, PIP, other accepted benefits or customers experiencing financial difficulty.

  • Free Discovery Enquiry: £0
  • Destination Discovery Plan: £29
  • Itinerary and Experience Planning Plan: £55
  • Complete Discovery and Planning Guidance Plan: £95

Revision wording

A minor revision means a small adjustment to the guidance provided. A major change, such as changing the destination, changing the dates completely, changing the traveller group or requesting a substantially different plan, may require a new request or an additional paid service.
