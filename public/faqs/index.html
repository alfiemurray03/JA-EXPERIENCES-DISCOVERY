How do I sign in?

Use JA Group Services ID from the Sign In page. The existing Microsoft Entra External ID flow securely returns you to the requested account or builder page.

Is there a permanent free plan?

No. Eligible customers may claim one 14-day free trial. Continued access requires an active paid plan.

When are tokens used?

The applicable token cost is shown by the builder. Opening a builder and reviewing a preview does not deduct tokens; the server records usage when a finished output is successfully saved.

Where do I find Builders?

Builders are a separate top-level service at /builders/. They are not part of the customer account portal.

How is my account protected?

Authentication uses JA Group Services ID, Microsoft Entra External ID, secure sessions and the existing account-security controls.

How do I make a data request?

Signed-in customers can use My Data & Privacy. Requests continue through the existing UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018 workflow.

Who handles activity bookings?

Bookings, payment, tickets, cancellation and refunds are handled by the named independent provider, such as GetYourGuide or Headout, under that provider’s terms.

How do I contact support?

Use the Contact page or the Support section in your customer account. Do not send passwords, payment card details or security codes.
