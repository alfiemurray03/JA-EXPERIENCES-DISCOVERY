What we do

A practical discovery and guidance service

We are not here to take over your trip. We help you understand your options, make sense of destinations and decide what to do next.

01

Destination discovery

Support choosing where to go by reviewing your interests, dates, budget, traveller type and general needs.

02

Activity guidance

Ideas for things to do, places to visit and experiences to consider through third-party providers.

03

Planning support

Written guidance, itinerary ideas, area notes and practical next steps delivered by email and PDF.

04

Affiliate signposting

Clear links to third-party activity providers, including Headout and GetYourGuide, where suitable.

How it works

Simple, controlled and human-led

The launch model remains manual where needed, so enquiries can be reviewed properly before any paid guidance work begins.

1. Send a free enquiry

Tell us what you are thinking about, including destination ideas, budget, dates, travellers and any support needs.

2. Receive a recommendation

We review your request and recommend whether a paid guidance plan is suitable.

3. Get your guidance

If you choose a paid plan, your written guidance is prepared and delivered by email and PDF.

Important boundaries

What JA Experiences & Discovery does not do

Clear boundaries help avoid confusion between JA guidance, third-party activity bookings and any future selected partner hotel model.

✕

No flights or transport

We do not sell, arrange or take payment for flights, visas, transfers, coaches, ferries, trains, taxis, cabs or car hire.

✕

No package holidays

We do not combine hotel rooms, transport, tours or activities into a package holiday or bundled travel arrangement.

✕

No provider guarantee

Third-party availability, pricing, cancellations, refunds and service delivery remain the responsibility of the relevant provider.

✓

You remain in control

Customers remain responsible for making and managing their own travel, transport and third-party provider arrangements.
