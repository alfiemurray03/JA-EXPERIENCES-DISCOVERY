What we do

Practical travel discovery and planning guidance.

JA Experiences & Discovery is not a package holiday company. We provide guidance, research and signposting so customers remain in control of their own arrangements.

01

Destination discovery

Support exploring destinations, city ideas, trip styles and practical considerations.

02

Experience ideas

Inspiration for activities, attractions, culture, family days out and local experiences.

03

Planning guidance

Written guidance to help you compare options, organise ideas and decide what to do next.
