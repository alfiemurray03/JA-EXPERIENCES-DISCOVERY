Service details

Built for practical self-service planning

JA Experiences & Discovery helps you create detailed plans while maintaining full control over your decisions and bookings.

Guided builders

Step-by-step builders help you create plans for family days out, travel experiences, special occasions, accessibility considerations, and budget planning.

All builders are self-service tools that generate planning outputs based on the information you provide.

Discovery tools

Discovery boards let you organize ideas, notes, budgets, and booking information in one place.

Use saved plans to revisit and update your outputs whenever you need.

What we don't do

We do not sell package holidays, arrange flights, arrange transport, apply for visas or ETIAS, manage third-party bookings, or handle third-party refunds.

Bookings and transactions with third-party providers are your responsibility.

Affiliate relationships

Some links may direct you to third-party providers. Bookings are made directly with those providers under their own terms, conditions, and privacy policies.

JA Group Services Ltd does not process payments or handle bookings for third-party services.
