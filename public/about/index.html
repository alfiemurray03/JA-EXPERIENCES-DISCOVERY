About the service

Destination discovery and experience guidance with a human point of view.

JA Experiences & Discovery is a trading division and service line of JA Group Services Ltd.

Our purpose

Make planning more manageable

We help customers compare destinations, organise experience ideas and turn a broad intention into practical next steps. The service is designed for different budgets, levels of confidence and support needs.

Our approach

Clear boundaries

JA provides research and guidance. Independent activity providers manage their own bookings. We do not sell flights, visas, transfers, transport or package holidays.

Our audience

Inclusive support

Our guidance can support families, carers, couples, solo travellers, disabled customers, customers with access needs and people who find online planning overwhelming.
