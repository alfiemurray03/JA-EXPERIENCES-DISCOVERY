Customer care

Complaints and concerns

We want concerns about JA's own guidance service to be reviewed fairly and promptly.

Complaint about JA

Email hello@jagroupservices.co.uk with your name, reference number, what happened and the outcome you are seeking.

We will acknowledge the complaint and provide a substantive response within a reasonable period, normally within 20 working days where possible.

Complaint about an activity booking

Contact the provider shown on the booking confirmation. GetYourGuide, Headout or the relevant supplier is responsible for its tickets, activity delivery, cancellations and refunds.

Data protection concern

Contact JA first so we can investigate. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office.

Emergency or in-destination issue

JA is not a 24-hour emergency service. Contact local emergency services, the provider, your insurer or official consular assistance as appropriate.
