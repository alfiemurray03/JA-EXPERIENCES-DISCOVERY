Future third-party partner options

Partner booking links and supplier details will be reviewed before wider public launch. No partner booking service is currently presented as live on this website.

How future partner links may work

JA Travel & Experiences may introduce selected links to established external providers. These links may help customers move from research and planning to a provider’s own website.

The external provider, not JA Group Services Ltd, will be responsible for the booking. Its terms, prices, availability, cancellation rules, amendment rules, customer support and complaints process will apply.

Before using a provider

  • Check the provider identity and product description.
  • Review the total price, currency and payment terms.
  • Read cancellation and amendment rules.
  • Confirm accessibility and suitability requirements.
  • Keep the provider’s booking confirmation and support details.

JA’s role

JA Group Services Ltd may provide discovery, planning, research and information support. It does not become the operator or supplier merely because a provider link appears on this website.
