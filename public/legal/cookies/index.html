Cookie policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

What cookies are

Cookies are small files or stored values used by websites to remember information, operate features or understand use. Similar technologies may perform related functions.

Current website use

The core JA Experiences & Discovery pages are designed to work without non-essential advertising or analytics cookies. Essential technical processing may be used by our hosting and security providers to deliver and protect the website.

Third-party content

Pages containing embedded third-party booking widgets, videos, maps or affiliate content may allow that provider to set cookies or collect technical information. Such content should be clearly identified. Where consent is legally required, non-essential technology should not load before that consent is obtained.

Managing cookies

You can use browser settings to block or delete cookies. Blocking essential technology may affect website operation. Provider websites have their own privacy and cookie information.

Future analytics

If JA introduces optional analytics, advertising or personalisation cookies, this policy and the website consent controls must be updated before those cookies are activated.
