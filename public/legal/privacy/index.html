Who is responsible for your information?

Information we may collect

  • Identity and contact details you provide.

Why we use it

We use information to answer enquiries, prepare quotes, enter and perform service contracts, deliver research, provide customer support, keep business records, prevent misuse, meet legal obligations and, where separately permitted, communicate relevant service updates.

Our lawful bases

Depending on the activity, we rely on steps requested before a contract, performance of a contract, legal obligations, legitimate interests in operating and protecting the business, consent, or explicit consent where special-category information is genuinely required. Disability or health information submitted through the enquiry form is optional and must be accompanied by the specific consent shown on that form.

Sharing

We may use trusted providers for email delivery, documents, hosting, payments or professional advice. This currently includes Cloudflare for website delivery and Resend for enquiry email delivery when the online form is used. They receive only information reasonably required for their role. We do not sell personal information. We will not send a customer's sensitive circumstances to a travel provider without a clear instruction or lawful reason.

International transfers

Some technology providers may process data outside the United Kingdom. Where relevant, we will use recognised safeguards and review provider terms.

Retention

Enquiry records that do not become customers will normally be reviewed and deleted when no longer needed. Contract, payment, complaint and accounting records may be retained for applicable legal and business periods. Sensitive planning information should be deleted or anonymised when no longer required.

Your rights

Depending on the circumstances, you may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict, object to or receive your information, and to withdraw consent. Contact us to make a request. You may also complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

Security

We use proportionate organisational and technical measures. Email is not an appropriate way to send passport copies, payment card details or extensive medical records. Please contact us before sending sensitive documents.

Updates

We may update this notice when our systems or services change. The date above shows the latest published version.
