Travel Insurance Notice

General pre-travel information for customers researching future journeys.

Customers should consider arranging suitable travel insurance through an authorised insurance provider.

JA’s position

JA Group Services Ltd does not currently advise on, arrange or sell travel insurance. Nothing on this website is a personal recommendation or regulated insurance advice.

Customer considerations

Customers may wish to consider cancellation, medical treatment, repatriation, baggage, activities, pre-existing medical conditions, destination risks and policy exclusions. Cover varies significantly, so policy documents and eligibility requirements should be reviewed carefully.

Official guidance

Customers should use an appropriately authorised insurance provider and seek suitable professional guidance where needed.
