Clear commercial disclosure

Affiliate links and independent providers

Some activity links and widgets may earn JA Group Services Ltd a commission after a qualifying booking.

What this means for you

When you follow an affiliate link or use a partner widget, you leave or interact with JA's website and book with the named third-party provider. The provider sets the price, availability, terms, cancellation rules and refund process.

Our role

JA Group Services Ltd provides discovery, signposting and guidance. We are not the tour operator, ticket issuer or activity provider for bookings completed through GetYourGuide, Headout or another named independent partner.

Commission

We may receive commission where a booking is correctly attributed and remains eligible under the partner's terms. This supports the operation of the website. Commission is not guaranteed and may be reversed for cancellations, refunds, disputes or tracking failures.

Partner priority

Headout is the planned primary activity affiliate partner and GetYourGuide is the secondary activity affiliate partner. Public integrations are introduced only after current terms, tracking and partner permissions have been checked.

Making a booking

Read the provider's current description, price, restrictions, accessibility information and cancellation terms before paying. Questions about an activity booking should be directed to the provider shown on the booking confirmation.
