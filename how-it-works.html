How it works

Simple discovery and planning guidance.

A clear route from free enquiry to structured guidance, while keeping third-party bookings and customer responsibilities separate.

A simple customer journey is being prepared.

The service is designed to keep enquiry, guidance and customer responsibility clear from the start.

01

Submit an enquiry

The Free Discovery Enquiry route will allow customers to explain what they are looking for before selecting any paid guidance option.

02

Select a guidance plan

Where suitable, customers may choose from planned paid guidance options with clear pricing and scope before purchase.

03

Receive guidance

Guidance is expected to be delivered by email and PDF at launch, allowing manual review and quality control.

Included direction

What the service may support

  • Destination ideas and discovery.
  • Activity and attraction suggestions.
  • Experience-led itinerary guidance.
  • Practical planning considerations.
  • Third-party activity signposting where appropriate.
Excluded services

What JA Group Services Ltd will not arrange

  • Flights, visas or transport bookings.
  • Package holidays or linked travel arrangements.
  • Transfers, taxis, trains, ferries or car hire.
  • Customer bookings with third-party activity providers.

Preparing for launch

Customer enquiry routes, paid guidance plans and selected partner hotel services are not yet open.
