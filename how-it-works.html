How it works

Simple discovery and planning guidance.

The service is being designed around clear enquiry, guidance and customer responsibility stages.

How the service is expected to work

The customer journey is being designed to remain clear, controlled and practical from first enquiry through to guidance delivery.

Step 1

Free enquiry

Customers will be able to submit an initial enquiry so the general nature of the request can be understood before any paid option is selected.

Step 2

Plan selection

Where suitable, customers may select a paid discovery or planning guidance plan. Pricing and scope will be shown before purchase.

Step 3

Email/PDF guidance

Guidance is expected to be delivered by email and PDF at launch, allowing manual review and careful quality control.

What we can help with

  • Destination ideas and discovery.
  • Activity and attraction suggestions.
  • Experience-led itinerary guidance.
  • Practical planning considerations.
  • Third-party activity signposting where appropriate.

What we do not arrange

  • Flights, visas or transport.
  • Package holidays or linked travel arrangements.
  • Transfers, taxis, trains, ferries or car hire.
  • Bookings with third-party activity providers.
