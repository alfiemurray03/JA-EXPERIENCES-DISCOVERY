About JA Experiences & Discovery.

A developing JA Group Services Ltd service line for destination discovery, experience guidance and practical planning support.

Built for discovery, guidance and clear signposting.

JA Experiences & Discovery is being developed as a service line of JA Group Services Ltd, supporting customers with practical destination and experience information.

01

Destination discovery

Helping customers explore destinations, cities, activity ideas and things to do before making their own arrangements.

02

Planning guidance

Preparing structured guidance options for customers who want clearer, organised suggestions by email and PDF.

03

Affiliate signposting

Clear third-party activity signposting may be provided, with proper affiliate disclosure and customer responsibility wording.

Current position

Pre-launch development

The service is currently being prepared for launch. Customer enquiries, paid guidance plans and selected partner hotel services are not yet open.

Further customer terms, service information and operational controls will be published before launch.

Operating boundary

Not a travel package service

JA Group Services Ltd does not sell, arrange or package holidays, flights, visas, transfers, trains, taxis, car hire or other transport bookings.

Customers remain responsible for their own travel and transport.
