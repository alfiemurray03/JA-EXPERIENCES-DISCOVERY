About JA Experiences & Discovery

JA Experiences & Discovery is a trading division/service line of JA Group Services Ltd. It provides online destination discovery, activity guidance, itinerary and experience planning support, affiliate signposting and customer support.

What it is

It is designed to help customers understand options, compare ideas and make more informed decisions before arranging their own travel, accommodation, activities or experiences.

It is operated by JA Group Services Ltd and is not a separate legal entity.

How it works

Services are delivered online. Guidance documents may be supplied by email in Word, Excel and/or PDF format where suitable.

Customers remain responsible for arranging and managing their own travel and transport.

Important points

  • It is intended to be practical, inclusive and accessible.
  • It may support families, carers, couples, solo travellers, customers on lower incomes and customers who feel overwhelmed by planning.
  • It provides guidance and signposting only unless a service is expressly stated otherwise.
