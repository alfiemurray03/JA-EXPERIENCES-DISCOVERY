About JA Experiences & Discovery

A developing service line focused on destination discovery, experience guidance and practical planning support.

About the service

JA Experiences & Discovery is a trading division and service line of JA Group Services Ltd. It is being developed to help customers explore destination ideas, activities, attractions and practical planning options.

The service is intended to provide guidance and signposting. It is not intended to operate as a package holiday provider, flight seller, transport arranger or travel agent.

Current status

The service is currently in pre-launch development. Further customer information, service terms, enquiry routes and operational controls will be published before full launch.

Paid guidance plans, customer hotel-room services and selected partner hotel information are not yet open.

Clear operating boundary

Customers remain responsible for arranging their own travel and transport. JA Group Services Ltd does not sell, arrange or package holidays, flights, visas, transfers, trains, taxis, car hire or other transport bookings.
