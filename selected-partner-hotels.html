Selected partner hotels

A future selected partner hotel model may be developed as a hotel-room-only service, separate from activity signposting and guidance plans.

No selected partner hotels are currently confirmed and no hotel rooms are currently available for customer purchase through JA Experiences & Discovery.

Required controls

Hotel customer payments must not be accepted unless written supplier authority, operational controls, customer terms, refund handling, reconciliation, insurance and accounting checks are in place.

No package travel

The selected partner hotel model must remain separate from activities, attractions, transport and affiliate bookings unless a further Board-approved legal, regulatory, VAT/accounting, insurance and operational review has been completed.
